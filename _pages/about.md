Dr. Sabrina Perry currently is an Assitant Professor in the [Department of Computer Science and Information Technology](https://csds.gsu.edu/) at [Austin Peay State University (APSU)](https://www.apsu.edu/). She received her Ph.D. in Computer Engineering (with a minor in Mathematics) from the University of Nebraska-Lincoln, NE, USA, 2022 and B.S. in Electronic and Information Engineering from Nanjing University of Posts and Telecommunications, Nanjing, China, 2014. Prior to joining APSU, she was an Instructor of Engineering Technology at in the Dept of Engineering Technology at Mississippi Valley State University (MVSU) from 2017-2025. 

Research Interests  
======
- Cybersecurity and Privacy
- Distributed and Explainable AI
- Internet of Things
- Cloud Computing and Edge Intelligence
- Cyber-physical Systems
- Wireless Networks
